Here's a timeline of Gerrard's Career:

  • 1998: First team debut

  • 2003: Liverpool Captain

  • 2004: Screamer against Olympiakos in Champions League

  • 2005: Won Champions League in Istanbul

  • 2006: Played a Major role in winning the FA cup final against Westham Scoring 25 yard screamer

  • 2012: Scored merseyside derby hat-trick against arch rivals Everton

  • 2014: Finished 2nd with Liverpool almost winning the EPL

  • 2015: Left Liverpool after his contract expired and moved to LA Galaxy

  • 2016: Retired from Professional Football

  • 2016: Returned to Liverpool as U18 coach
